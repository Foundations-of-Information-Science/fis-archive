Communication through language is perhaps the main characteristic of
the dynamics of human society as it is the feature that makes
possible the gradual integration of the INFORMATION as knowledge
accumulated needed by humans for making better any decision

This general knowledge empirically, heuristically or scientifically
integrated for any community (local, regional, national or
international) as cultural, economic, social, political,Š information
is indeed indispensable for learning to push ahead the evolvement of
causal interactions among people and organizations in order to
improve their living and their dynamic properties respectively.

Most humans individually keep trying to participate in the evolvement
of every societal organization through ideas transferred by means of
spoken and /or written language. These ideas being engendred by the
intended comprehension and assimilation of idealized cultural values
under the influence of feelings, emotions, susceptibilities,
sentiments, passions,Š that emerge in every brain after being
perceived and interpreted, always peculiarly the surrounding reality.

So far so good, if it were prudent and sensible to idealize the
behavior of the humans that will be involved in each case in order to
create a reliable framework as needed for impulsing the development
of well chosen causal interactions.

The trajectory determined by humans for the whole civilizing
adventure shows evidently that at the dawn of one conventional third
millennium IT IS NOT ADVISABLE, therefore IT IS NOT FEASIBLE to
believe that the performance of humans nowadays could be idealized
for creating a humane perspective for humankind neither for
increasing the survival chances of Homo sapiens sapiens. It seems
convenient to examine some arguments about it.

Very often all along the last millennia some knowledge has caused the
degeneration of many terrestrial circumstances due to the narrow
minded and shortsighted decision makers' interests and motivations
which were -and still are - conceived, designed, structured and
implemented under the influence and even pressure of the unilateral
ambition and the selfish, egoistic and greedy thoughts that arise in
brains exclusively oriented by the desire of increasing the power and
the domain of certain individuals and their vassals, accomplices and
partners.

Evidently it has been the communication among human specimens, who
have learnt to perform inhumanely what has caused the controversial
manifestation of the whole civilizing trajectory, which has been
until today essentially determined by all kinds of sanguinary actions
supported by pseudo-cultural values that were invented for justifying
these actions and organized with the support of informationthat was
invented and propagated for such a purpose

Many other animals are also able to generate ideas and to organize
their individual performance in sccordance with their thinking, but
cannot communicate any idea to the other living beings located in
their surrroundings because they lack words. Therefore they only
manage from time to time to influence other animals at a distance and
occasionally to affect the behavior of some of them through peculiar
signs such as the movement of the limbs, the attitudes if the body,
the expression of the face,Š Consequently none of the other animals
have sttitudes against the species they belong to.

Animals will never organize a war against their congeners

Humans instead are even encouraged by means of communication of ideas
to be increasingly motivated till becoming deeply interested in
learning to behave more and more inhumanely in front of humans and
also animals
