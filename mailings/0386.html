Dear FISers,

Briefly responding to the 5 mess. on the "disconnected" suggestions, I am
very glad hearing the positive ressonances in Ted and James--and also
welcome the criticisms by Edwina and Norbert, and Gyuri's focusing
comments. My limited suggestions were never produced trying to set up a
partiture for the whole fis "orchestra", but as an invitation to interested
parties of our "jazz band" to try to play a tune together. Apart from our
solo playings, we really need collective tunes (please, note the
plural!!!). So the critics or any other parties are invited to contribute
with their collective tunes too --and we could exchange fragmentes,
melodies, invited players, etc.

My impression is that the coming e-conf. could open an exciting
oportunity-window for the whole fis project (including sci. projects,
financing,fis institutes, etc.), provided that we encounter an appropiate
balance between uncohesive & cohesive trends.

And, needless to say, other FISers interested in exploring the info "zero
system" for the next e-conf. will be welcome to the present mini-band of
three solo players.

Best wishes

Pedro
