The civilizing processes conceived, structured and implemented
through discovery and invention of information have culminated in the
manifestation of an HOMOSPHERE that has been continuously growing
without being noticed that the carrying capacity of the terrestrial
Nature is finite, and assuming de facto that the existence of human
life could be considered GRANTED FOR EVER.

However the civilization is a fact whether we liked or not; whether
some humans justify its existence by means of reasoning while others
question its manifestation also by means of reasoning, which becomes
in both cases the subject that motivates communication.

Therefore it couldn't be denied that OUR magnificent and
controversial WESTERN civilization, the only one that prevails at
present - seriously criticized and also seriously defended by means
of terrorism ideologically conceived and technologically organized -
is the environment where every one of the vast majority of humans
(99.99%) essays day after day to vindicate his or her presence in
time and in space.

Due to perhaps obvious reasons most humans search almost tireless,
despite many failures, the proper information - biased by left or
right or central or green or dogmatic ideologies or even one
encouraged by fundamentalism - that may allow each human to organize
consciously his or her immediate survival, being ready though not
sufficiently aware about the kind of available information that would
allow everyone individually to react spontaneously and even very
often successfully when being confronted to unexpected difficult,
risky and very dangerous situations.

So far so good say many humans who assume that all what matters is to
try to be happy enough while living miserably or surrounded by all
civilized facilities or stealing things and ideas or killing people
and animals for pleasure or in the name of a certain divine
personality or taking unilateral advantage of everything found in the
surroundings, etc.

What kind of information uses every human for being happy?

Are humans entitled to generated or invent information for learning
through experimentation what might be or should be the role of humans
on Earth or what kind of contribution could be expected from Homo
sapiens sapiens - comprised the loquens one - for leading somewhere,
therefore purposefully, the evolutionary possibilities on this unique
planet.

Today the INFORMATION accumulated through diverse kinds of
communication among humans shows that the whole civilizing adventure
has become:

* a controversial concern, as it is interpreted diversely even
by every brain during the time everyone is alive;

* a contradictory affair as most humans reject, rather
childishly, the savage wildness of the whole Nature while building
more and more aberrant urban environments where human life can be
efficiently organized without being noticed that NATURE was. Is and
will be the main circumstance that makes possible the presence of
humans on Earth, our unique planet as there is not any other space in
the whole Universe for these frankly arrogant beings;

* a dangerous informational framework as it intends to justify
the need of a globalized warfare as it has been cleverly organized by
highly civilized fellows during the last twentieth century until
today. It is very valuable information that would be nor advisable
neither sensible to ignore: the First World War (1914 -1918) and the
Second World War (1939 -1945) as childish and mature searching of a
new world order that would allow the highly civilized countries to
distribute among them the other countries that were considered as
primitive, barbarous or poorly civilized communities. The Third World
War, an economic warfare organized and implemented during the postwar
period (1950s - 1990s) by winners and losers of the second one. It
was not even declared but it was quite successful because it allowed
empowered countries (supported by capitalistic or socialist
ideologies) by means of Research and Development policies to continue
taking advantage unilaterally of natural resources located anywhere
on the planet causing all the other countries (identified by
definition as less developed ones) to become poorer and poorer. The
Fourth World War, an economic, political and cultural warfare
conceived for modernizing the previous colonization through the
development of information and communication technology, and
announced through powerful mass media as the unique possibility of
organizing faster and faster everything related with the needs of
human way of life (transportation, communications, data processing,
internet 1995,...) in modern times. It has aimed at destroying
borders, governments and nationalisms because they are not longer
necessary, but it has become already the source of globalization of
unemployment, criminality, misery, corruption, hypocrisy,... The
possible Fifth World War, a warfare that seems to be inspired and
encouraged by some divinities. It is the first one that has been
already conceived in the 21st century, which apparently emerged
unexpectedly, though perhaps it confirms humankind the announced
apocalypse during several millennia after being recognized that a
certain god - necessarily a divine one - has chosen to protect and
support a certain number of people for fighting (till winning)
against another group of people also protected and supported by
another divine god.
