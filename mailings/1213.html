Dear FIS Colleagues,
 
I enjoyed your last postings and significant additions to the main
topic. Actually, our discussion was about several different topics, or
maybe several aspects of the same vast topic...not easy, but very
interesting. As Loet says, new dimensions may come out, the exploration
of which is certainly very stimulating. I wish we had the opportunity to
talk about these aspects in a meeting, face by face, for a longer
available time. Maybe this can be done in the future, as a side
development of the FIS discussion list initiative. The presence of some
disagreement is not a problem, it is instead a richness of the
scientific community, if we are able to keep (as we did, on my opinion)
the discussion on tracks of clarity and broad-minded research.
 
I therefore would like to thank all of you for this intellectual
exchange opportunity and look forward to future meetings, either
personal and virtual.
 
My warmest wishes for the holiday season and the New Year.
