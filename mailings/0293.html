The current issue of Cybernetics & Human Knowing (just published) is a
special issue entitled "The Embodied Mind and the Baldwin Effect" and is
the result of an interdisciplinary meeting convened last year to discuss
the integration of evolutionary origins of mind and consciousness with
emerging principles of developmental and evolutionary biology.

Contributors include Soren Brier, Terrence Deacon, David Depew, Kalevi
Kull and Bruce Weber.

Contents, abstracts and selected full text on:
http://www.imprint.co.uk/cyber.html
