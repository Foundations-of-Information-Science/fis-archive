Dear all

Here is a conference that may interest several of you!

Within an attempt to find solutions to today's World Crisis the 1st IEEE International Conference on Industrial Informatics - INDIN03 to be held in Banff, Canada, August 21-24, 2003 [http://www.enel.ucalgary.ca/INDIN03/] will bring together a blend of academic and industry leaders into a synergetic forum for discussion and exchange of ideas, under the Logo: e-Logistics for a Fail-Safe World.

If you are or are ready to get involved into the most acute Information and Communications Technologies R&D area supporting today's dynamics through innovative solutions for avoiding surprise and emergency response management in all areas of our life, please join us in the beautiful and peaceful environment of Banff National Park [http://www.banffcentre.ca/] and let your spirit vibrate with ours in synergetic creativity.

We have arranged a bouquet of the best reputable Keynote Speakers (among which: Prof. Lotfi A. Zadeh, U C Berkeley, Prof. Hojjat Adeli, The Ohio State University, Prof. Tharam Dillon, La Trobe University Australia, Bill St Arnaud from CANARIE, Alan Martel, President IMS Canada, Prof. Soren Brier from Copehagen Business School etc.) and the technical issues will be deepened by Tutorials (by researchers from Rockwell Automation, US, KU Leuven, Belgium, Berkeley Initiative in Soft Computing, US and Tampere University of Technology in Finland). David Johnstone, President and CEO of Cornerstone Strategies, Calgary will deliver the Opening Keynote Address.

We have arranged an excellent deal with the Banff Centre that will include breakfast and lunch as well as a major percentage from the Banquet (held at the Centre as well) in the room cost. Therefore we strongly recommend that you stay at the Conference hotel.

For an extended stay in our beautiful Province of Alberta - we recommend that you consider as well attending the International Conference of Intelligent Agent Technologies, Calgary, Alberta, August 27-29, 2003 [http://www.iiasr.org/ATS/index.php]. We will try to arrange for a joint Conference Fee for those of you wanting to attend both events.

Technical Tours to Calgary's ICT focal points: TR Labs; iCORE Labs; Faculty of Engineering at The University of Calgary, etc. will highlight your visit here.

Recreational possibilities abound in August in the Rocky Mountains! (Everything will be posted on the Conference website.)

For more details see attached Call for Papers.
