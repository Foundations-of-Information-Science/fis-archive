Dear colleagues,
     you are invited to participate to the SCI 2004 conference, which will
take
place in Orlando, Florida (July 18-21, 2004), by sending an extended abstract
(500 to 1500 words) or a draft paper (2000 to 5000 words), for the session
entitled "Agent Based Methodologies Applied to Complex Social Systems,
Economics, Business, Finance and Enterprise Management". The deadline is
December, 10th, 2003; all the accepted papers will be published in the
conference proceedings and the 10% best of them also in the SCI scientific
journal.

You can read about the conference at the following address:

http://www.iiisci.org/sci2004/WebSite/default.asp

and here you find the sessions:

http://www.iiisci.org/sci2004/InvitedSession/InvitedSessionPre.asp

Please send your abstract to remond@di.unito.it and add author(s) and/or co-
author(s) names, addresses, telephone and fax numbers, and e-mail addresses.

Deadline: December, 10th
Submit to: remond@di.unito.it

Please forward this message to all the people who could be interested. Thanks
in advance for your attention and best regards,

Marco Remondino.

Department of Computer Science,
University of Turin, Italy.
