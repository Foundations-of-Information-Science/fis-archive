Dear Gordana,

Thanks for your interesting, valuable comments. May I suggest that
chemists, and physicists working with materials like transistors, do,
indeed, construct material objects, to ordered specifications, by
building layers of atoms according to a plan. It’s even possible today,
at the most minute level, to position a single atom with some useful
property at a particular position among a lattice of other types of atoms.
