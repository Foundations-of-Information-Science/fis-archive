Dear colleagues,

Good news from Elsevier. The production troubles of the special issue
"based on" the Vienna conference are definitely over --the final reference
is BioSystems, 46, 1-2, 1998. (please, note that they are not the official
"proceedings", as someone has recently asked me).

Contributing authors will receive a complimentary copy from Elsevier quite
soon (the first authors).

Everyone who has seen the issue tells that it looks damned good --thanks to
the brilliant papers sent by the contributing authors, but also
particularly thanks to the very elegant editorial work performed by Kevin
Kirby (with some cooperation from Koichiro Matsuno, Michael Conrad, and
myself).

Hope you will enjoy the issue.

greetings
