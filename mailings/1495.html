Dear Pedro,

thank you for your quick reply to Sergei. He is much interested
in the meaning of entropy in the non-equilibrium quantum case.
Although the correspondence is not perfect, most people believe
that in the quantum case the Wigner function plays the role of
the probability. Unfortunately, the Wigner function is not
always positive which implies that the traditional formulation
of entropy S=-\sum_i p_i \ln(p_i) has to be revised, otherwise
there appears the logarithm of negative arguments.

I hope that Sergei and perhaps other people of the forum can
contribute to these exciting problems.

Best, Thorsten
