Dear FISers,

The FIS2005 Organizing Committee is very pleased to announce
that the Third Conference on the Foundations of Information Science
will be held in Paris the 4-7 July 2005.

You are invited to submit abstracts and/or papers for oral
communications and posters.

Topics, instructions for authors, deadlines, provisional programme,
and other useful informations are available on the FIS2005 website:
http://www.mdpi.org/fis2005
(see also pointer in http://www.mdpi.org/entropy/conferences.html)

We hope to hear soon from you.
