Subj: Symmetry Festival 2006

Dear Colleague,

I am very pleased to inform you that the Symmetry Festival 2006
will be held in Budapest, 12-18 August 2006.

The Conference is transdisciplinary: science, education, art.
Please visit the site: http://www.conferences.hu/symmetry2006

Deadline for submitting an abstract or extended abstract: 15 March 2006
(use the web form; full papers may be submitted via email in PDF format).

Questions should be addressed to the Conference Secretariat
(Ms. Eva Thiry: thiry@sztaki.hu), or to the Conference chair:
Prof. Gyorgy Darvas (h492dar@ella.hu).
