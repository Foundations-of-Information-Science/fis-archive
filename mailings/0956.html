Dear All:

I have worked hard to establish the relationship between information
and diversity. Diversity of political systems and human races is beautiful.
System full of diversity is beautiful. System full of symmetry is less
diverse and is ugly. I have argued in a paper at : http://www.mdpi.org/ijms/papers/i2010010.pdf .

Diversity means tolerance of differences and coexistence of different beings.

I like a word of Thomas Jefferson's First Inaugural Address very much:
But every difference of opinion is not a difference of principle.

I even tried to launch a journal entitled DIVERSITY (http://www.prodiversity.org/).

Shu-Kun
