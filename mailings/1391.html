FIS friends,

About the ongoing exchanges (thanks to Bob and Aleks for introducing us to
their views), I have just met an intriguing quotation from Heinz Pagels:

"The visible world is neither matter nor spirit, but the invisible
organization of energy."

One wonders what would Pagels say, today, about the brave new world of
'dark matter' and 'dark energy' (the Fifth force out of the vacuum) around
20 times larger than the visible world.

The new physics of information, so crucial for integrating a new cosmology,
looks at the same time a necessary step in the advancement of a whole
informational approach --and is deeply related with the discussion on
entropy/information that Michel will start next Monday!
