HOW TO ENJOY A QUIET CHRISTMASS

Given that a couple of colleagues have asked me their disconnection during
the present Christmass, the best thing is that each one controls his/her
own presence by sending the corresponding command control to:

                              listserver@listas.unizar.es

simply with the message:

UNSUBSCRIBE FIS 

--
And, remember, when you whish reconnect:
SUSBCRIBE FIS your_name
--
Felices Fiestas!
