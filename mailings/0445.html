Dear colleagues,

Our conference web pages http://www.mdpi.net/fis2002/ are already receiving
files (right now there are 7). Some are real papers, others are extended
abstracts. Tomorrow at least 4 more papers will be added.

Everyone interested is encouraged to send at least a brief abstract during
the next two weeks (until 2nd may, or even further if the chairs allow
him/her). The new structure of the web pages (after great work done by Sen
Lin) allows a very efficient registration, login, and browsing procedures.
Any registered user can now modify or susbtitute his own paper on the spot.

People withouth computer expertise (eg, they can only send an attached
document in Word and are unable to handle html stuff) will directly send
their paper or their extended abstract to Sen Lin (senlin@mdpi.org) or to
me. But before, please, they shoud try to follow the electronic way.

Have an exciting conference!
